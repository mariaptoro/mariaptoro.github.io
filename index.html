About Me

I am a biologist specializing in the behavioral and evolutionary ecology of amphibians, with a strong focus on animal communication and acoustic monitoring. My research aims to understand how environmental factors influence amphibian behavior and biodiversity.

Research Interests

  • Behavioral and Evolutionary Ecology of Amphibians
  • Acoustic Monitoring and Animal Communication
  • Effects of Noise Pollution on Biodiversity
  • Climate and Sound Interactions in Ecosystems
